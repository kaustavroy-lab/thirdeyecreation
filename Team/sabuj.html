Sabuj Singh
I am the founder of The Third Eye Creation, a one stop solution to all of your photo and video related worries and a freelance photographer. I attended to Kendriya Vidyalaya Tarakeswar for secondary schooling and to Panisheola Indira Smriti Vidyapith for senior secondary. For the professional requirement, I have completed Diploma in Still Photography from Asian Academy of Film and Television, in Noida. I have a work experience in this field for six years.
I had been a web casting invigilator for the Loksabha Election 2019. I had worked as a photographer at Khawai, Wandrous Events and also in some notable college fests like Umang, Utsav and Pulse. Even I worked as photographer at several events. Some notable mentions are ‘The 550th Birth Anniversary of Guru Nanak’ hosted by the Punjab Government and Yoga Day celebration at CP Delhi. Currently I am a D.O.P at Dristikon Online and I-Nomadic.
